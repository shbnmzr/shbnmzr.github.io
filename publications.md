## Thesis

### Graph-meta: Improving Metagenomic Classification with Graph-based Learning

**Author:** Shabnam Zareshahraki  
**Advisor:** Dr. Matteo Comin  
**Institution:** Università degli Studi di Padova  
**Completed:** April 2026  
**[GitHub](https://github.com/shbnmzr/graph-meta)** | **[Paper](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=ck3B1P0AAAAJ&citation_for_view=ck3B1P0AAAAJ:u-x6o8ySG0sC)** 

Master's thesis proposing a hybrid GNN-based architecture for taxonomic classification of metagenomic sequences. The work combines deep learning with graph-structured representations of genomic assembly graphs, outperforming state-of-the-art sequence-based classifiers on real-world multi-omic benchmarks.
